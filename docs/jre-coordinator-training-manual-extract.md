Introduction to the Manual
Welcome to the team! Read on for important information and a concise overview of your job responsibilities. These will be subdivided into weekly, monthly, as needed, and annual tasks. As with any community based organization, this position will be flexible based on the growing and changing needs of the community, but if you follow the general guidelines presented in this text you will have a solid foundation in how to support the work of the JRE.

Donorsnap and Donor Acknowledgements
Enter all check donations into Donorsnap, then put them in a designated envelope for deposit. Leave the envelope on the desk and tell Rabbi Oratz they are there. Make sure you note for yourself what date you are up to.
Enter all credit card donations into Donorsnap. You can find these at Authorize.net. Make sure you note for yourself what date you are up to.
Issue Thank You notes. I like to do this one by one, so I know I don't miss anyone. I enter a donation into Donorsnap, then fill out the Thank You Note- Single Donation Word template.
To find addresses, you can use Donorsnap, or click the transaction ID number in Authorize.net for more information about the donor. 
File the Thank you for printing- I have a To Print folder in Dropbox. File the completed Thank You with the last name of the donor and the date you created the thank you (for example, Warhit 6.13) in the To Print folder. You can then exit the document, and click the template on your desktop to make a new one (I love templates, in case you can’t tell)
When you are finished entering all the donations into Donorsnap, and you have finished creating all the Thank You notes and filing them in To Print, you can print them. The best way to do this is by selecting all of the files in the To Print folder, then right click and select print. Make sure you have the heavy fancy paper in the printer first.
When all the documents in To Print are printed, move them to that year’s Thank Yous folder in Dropbox.  
Make address labels at Avery online using the template printed on the package of the clear labels. Follow all printing instructions carefully. 
Leave the Thank Yous on the desk and tell Rabbi Oratz to sign them. 
Once signed, fold each Thank you into thirds, insert into a matching heavy fancy envelope, stick on a clear address label, stamp, and a clear JRE address label. Seal with crystal clear tape and put the stack into the mailbox next to the traffic light where you go when you drive out of the parking lot (or any mailbox that is convenient for you).

Monthly Tasks

Quickbooks
Open the Citibank statement for this month. 
Open Quickbooks Online. Click on banking. Match each transaction to the correct vendor and categorize it. This will get easier over time as the names become familiar. If you are ever unsure or the statement doesn't add up to the Quickbooks report, ask Rabbi Oratz about it.
If you have a new vendor, add them to the system as a Vendor (we use it for everyone). 

Viventium (Payroll)
Get a Viventium username if you haven’t already, and make sure you are entered as a new employee into the system. Call and ask them to walk you through exactly how to do Payroll.
By mid month, you should ask Rabbi Oratz if there are any changes to be made to payroll. Make those changes (call Viventium for instructions), and make notes if they are temporary. 
Start payroll in the payroll dashboard tab. Click Payroll preview and make sure all info is correct. The only employees who get paid using Viventium are the W2 employees (you and the Rabbis), so it makes sense that the majority of the people who are employees do not receive money with payroll, you can ignore that notification. They are there for 1099s during tax season. 
Ignore the notification that the check date doesn't match up with the pay period- we pay on the first of the month for the month ahead, which is unusual.
If all of the information is correct, you can submit payroll. IMPORTANT: make sure to submit payroll on time or early to make sure you don’t delay the funds going out on time. 
If you make a mistake, call Viventium right away.
If you are late on payroll, let everyone know.
